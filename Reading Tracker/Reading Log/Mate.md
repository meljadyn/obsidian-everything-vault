(Review written a couple months later in June 2026)

Another solid book. I tore through it, so I clearly can’t complain, but it also didn’t last super well for me. I liked it better than Bride, but the fact that her *terminal illness* was *actually going into heat* was the worst plot twist I’ve ever seen and I never fully forgave it for that. 

Other than that, it was a solid story and I liked that she knew how to cook.
