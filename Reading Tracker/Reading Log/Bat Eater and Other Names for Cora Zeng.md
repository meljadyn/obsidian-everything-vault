I enjoyed this read. It’s been a month since I read it, but I really enjoyed Cora’s PoV and the whole concept of Hungry Ghosts. I was fairly enraptured by this read, by Cora’s compulsions, by the ghosts, and by the reveal of the killer. I did start to care about the side characters as well, though a little later than I would have preferred.
