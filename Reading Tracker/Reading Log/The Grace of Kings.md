(Review written several months later, in June 2026)

I really enjoyed this read. I was in a reading slump, but I loved the main male character (his name is escaping me) and the writing style. The *writing style*. It was engaging and enrapturing. I loved the POVs we had. On paper, the view we had of the characters feel further away than most narratives, but in practice, I adored it. But the reading slump won, and I wasn’t giving it the attention this book deserves. I really look forward to when I can pick this one up again.
